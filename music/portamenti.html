A young note with a curious timbre ventures into the glistening Gulf of Portamenti. Falling off beat, the note is engulfed in harmony. He isn't alone. The gulf is full of resonant creatures such as the deep-water bass and the chromatic carps. Caught by a gust through his pipes, the medium is too sharp for the curious note once he finds that all tunes have an ending.

This composition is a venture into complex harmony, low frequencies, and instrumental interactions. One of my favorite endings. Probably broke my headphones creating it. Might break yours too.

Instruments: piano, harp, clarinet, bass guitar, vibraphone, tom-tom, tam-tam, organ(s)?
