What is Protistory?

It is what it sounds like. The story of the protists. You're part of this story and so are plants and fungi. But here I will educate on Unicellular Eukaryotes but in an unconventional way. Protistory brings ideas of science, history, microscopy, art, and Earth models all in one interdisciplinary space. A holistic protist education. This project is currently being developed.
